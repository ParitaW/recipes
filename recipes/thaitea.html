Thai Tea

Thai tea is a beloved traditional beverage from Thailand, known for its distinctive orange color and rich, creamy taste. This sweet and refreshing drink is made from strongly-brewed Ceylon tea, flavored with star anise, crushed tamarind, and other spices. It's traditionally served over ice and topped with evaporated milk or coconut milk, creating a beautiful layered effect. Popular both in Thailand and internationally, it's the perfect complement to spicy Thai dishes or enjoyed on its own as a refreshing treat.

Ingredients

  • Ceylon tea leaves
  • Star anise
  • Tamarind
  • Cardamom
  • Cloves
  • Condensed milk
  • Evaporated milk

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add Ceylon tea leaves, star anise, tamarind, cardamom, and cloves.
  3. Simmer for 5-10 minutes to infuse the flavors.
  4. Strain the tea mixture into a pitcher.
  5. Add condensed milk to sweeten.
  6. Stir well and let cool.
  7. Fill glasses with ice and pour the tea over.
  8. Top with evaporated milk or coconut milk.
  9. Stir gently to create a layered effect.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
